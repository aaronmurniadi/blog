Welcome to my Typesettings collection! In my free time, I enjoy recreating
document layouts for fun. As I read, I’m often so captivated by the design of
books and articles that I feel inspired to try and reproduce their look myself.
I used <a href="https://www.latex-project.org/">LaTeX</a> a lot before 2024, now I
exclusively use <a href="https://typst.app/">Typst</a>.

Note: You can click on the images to view in PDF format.
